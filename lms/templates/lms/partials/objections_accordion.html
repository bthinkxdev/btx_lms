Common questions (honest answers)

Will I really earn more with this?
Prompt skills don’t guarantee income, but they reduce time on tasks, improve output quality, and help you pitch and interview better. Freelancers and employed grads in our cohort typically report faster workflows and clearer deliverables; your results depend on how you apply the prompts.
Is this beginner-friendly?
Yes. Day 1 starts from zero. You only need curiosity and basic English. If you can use email and Google, you can follow the program. Daily quizzes keep you on track, no skipping ahead.
What if I fail the quizzes or exam?
You can retake day quizzes to unlock the next module. The final exam is scored by the team; if you don’t pass, you can inquire about a retake policy. Your progress and videos stay available while you’re enrolled.
